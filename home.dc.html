Exercise Physiology

Real, lasting recovery — built around you

Our Clinical Exercise Physiologists are university-qualified allied health professionals who use exercise as medicine — for injury recovery, chronic pain, chronic disease and everyday function. We look at how your body responds to movement, then build a program around your goals, not a generic template.

Whether you're recovering from surgery, managing a workers compensation claim, living with a chronic condition, or simply want to move with more confidence — our team meets you where you are.
