What is exercise physiology?

Exercise, prescribed like medicine

Exercise physiologists are university-qualified allied health professionals who specialise in delivering exercise for the prevention and management of injuries and chronic disease. We study how the healthy — and the injured or unhealthy — body responds to movement, and use that science to guide every program we write.

All Agility Rehabilitation exercise physiologists are accredited with Exercise & Sport Science Australia (ESSA), our profession's peak body — so you can be confident you're in qualified, evidence-based hands.
