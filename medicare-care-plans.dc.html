Medicare / Care Plans

Subsidised sessions through your GP care plan

If you're living with a chronic health condition or injury, you may be eligible for Medicare benefits towards Exercise Physiology. A referral from your GP — a Chronic Disease Management Plan (EPC) — gives access to subsidised sessions each calendar year.
